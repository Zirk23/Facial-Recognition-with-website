About Mad Plate's Sizzling House


Mad Plate Sizzling House is a family-owned sizzling restaurant by Cabe Family located at Sta. Ana, Manila. Established on March 10, 2023, it is a newly started business and they provide a wide range of meals, including Sizzling dishes, Silog meals, and Flavoured chicken. Pick up food or “pulutan” that is eaten by beer drinkers is also one of the foods that they offer since the vicinity of the restaurant is near resto and KTV bars.
